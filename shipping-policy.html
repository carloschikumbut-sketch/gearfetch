Shipping Logistics

From Us to your destination. We provide secure, authenticated delivery for every single order. Delivering nationwide, our goal is to offer you the best delivery options, no matter where you live. Every day, we deliver to our customers across Zimbabwe, ensuring that we provide the very highest levels of responsiveness to you at all times.

Rates & Timelines

The time frame for order delivery is divided into two parts - Processing Time & Shipping Time:

Processing Time

As soon as order is confirmed, please allow a few hours for your order to be processed and be ready to be delivered. This includes Order verification, tailoring, quality check and packaging.

Delivery Time

After processing and leaving our warehouse, you will be notified when your orders are off for delivery. Orders usually take a few hours in Harare and out of Harare you will be notified about the pickup time at selected pickup points. You will also be able to check order status after you create an account and you login on the Gear Fetch Store.

Policy & Satisfaction

Returns &
Exchanges

We stand behind our collection. If your product isn't perfect, we've designed a straightforward process to get it right.

The Guarantee

You have 3 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return or exchange.

All items must be returned in Deadstock Condition—unworn, with original packaging fully intact.

How to Return

01

Initiate Request

Contact our support team with your order number and reason for return.

02

Verification

Wait for a return authorization. We usually respond within 24 hours.

03

Secure Shipping

Pack the product securely in a double-box to protect the original box. Once you are ready to ship your return, address your package to shipping address which will be provided by our support team.

04

Return Shipping Method

Use a trackable and insured shipping method to return your package, with your order number clearly printed on your shipping label.

05

Refund Processing

Once your return is authenticated, please allow 24-72 hours for your bank to credit the refund back to your original payment method.

06

Final Notification

You will receive a final confirmation email once the transaction is completed on our end.

Important Notice

Gear Fetch is not responsible for any return shipping fees, or for returns that are lost or stolen while in transit. We strongly recommend using a trackable and insured shipping method with a signature requirement.
